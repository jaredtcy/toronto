3D2N Toronto Itinerary

Dates: 30 May (Fri) – 1 June (Sun)

Arrival: 30 May, 6:40 AM
Departure: 1 June, Flight at 1:55 PM

Hotel: Union Hotel, York Street (breakfast included)

Day 1: Explore Toronto (30 May, Friday)

  • 07:30 – Arrive at Union Hotel, York Street (store luggage or early check-in)
  • 08:00 – Breakfast at Cafe Landwer or O&B Canteen (near TIFF Bell Lightbox)
  • 09:00 – University of Toronto campus walk or visit Royal Ontario Museum
  • 11:30 – Explore Kensington Market and Chinatown
  • 13:00 – Lunch at Seven Lives Tacos or KINKA Izakaya
  • 14:00 – Visit CN Tower and optionally Ripley's Aquarium
  • 16:00 – Walk along Harbourfront Centre / Queens Quay
  • 18:30 – Dinner at 360 The Restaurant at the CN Tower
  • Optional: Sunset at Polson Pier

Day 2: Niagara Falls (Self-Guided) (31 May, Saturday)

  • 07:00 – Hotel breakfast
  • 07:45 – Head to Union Station

Getting There: Take GO Train/Bus combo from Union Station:

  • 08:51 – Depart Union Station
  • 11:07 – Arrive Niagara Falls
  • Buy GO Transit Weekend Day Pass

Niagara Attractions (11 AM – 5 PM)

  • Floral Clock
  • Table Rock Welcome Centre & Horseshoe Falls
  • Floral Showhouse
  • Optional: Niagara Parkway walk or Hornblower boat ride

Lunch: Table Rock House Restaurant or casual dining near the falls

  • 17:45 – Depart Niagara Falls
  • 20:08 – Arrive back at Toronto Union Station
  • Dinner near hotel (Figo, Rosalinda, or light bite)

Day 3: Chill Morning & Departure (1 June, Sunday)

  • 07:00 – Hotel breakfast
  • 07:45 – Visit St. Lawrence Market or Distillery District
  • 09:30 – Return to hotel, grab luggage
  • 10:30 – Leave for Pearson Airport (UP Express or Uber)
Note: Weather in late May is mild (15–22°C). Wear comfy shoes and bring a light jacket. Niagara WEGO bus day pass available (~$10).
